My bachelor’s thesis focused on exploring the possibility of procedurally generating enemies. The result is a rogue-like shooter game in which the player fights against robots that are dynamically assembled during gameplay from modular blocks, with each block serving a specific function.

The robots are controlled by AI trained through machine learning.
The thesis includes a detailed analysis of L-systems, during which I had to formally define and implement a custom system called the KTL-System to suit the project’s needs.
For AI training, the Unity ML-Agents plugin was used.
