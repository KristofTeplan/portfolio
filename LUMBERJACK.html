Lumberjack Escape is board game 1v1 where players try to reach other side of map by building paths and using action cards. There is a strong emphasis on sabotaging the opponent.

Full print and play kit can be downloaded here:
https://github.com/KristofTeplan/Lumberjack-Escape

Game was designed in colaboration with Patrik Potančok and ​Ines del Transito Vaquero Ramirez

I worked on design and most of the rules, playtesting and manufacturing of prototypes. I contributed in visual side just with some corrections and backgrounds.
