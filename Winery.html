Practical project in collaboration with Roland Schulz during my bachelor's studies at VUT FIT.

The project is available on GitHub. For more information read documentation.

It was developed for Víno Marcinčák, a. s., and focuses on simulating a grape processing line for wine production. The results were used to support a purchasing decision for an additional machine with the capacity determined by our simulation.

In this project, I was responsible for designing the Petri net and implementing most of the simulation logic. I also contributed to documentation and the development of visualization tools.
